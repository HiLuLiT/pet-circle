# Future Features

Ideas and features to consider for future development. Items here are not committed to a timeline.

---

## 1. Responsive Design

Adapt the UI to work well across different screen sizes and form factors — phones, tablets (iPad), and potentially web. Includes flexible layouts, breakpoint-aware components, and proper scaling of typography and spacing.

---

## 2. AI Chat

An AI-powered chat assistant within the app that can help pet owners with questions about their pet's health data, medication schedules, respiratory rate trends, and general pet care guidance. Could integrate with the existing measurement and medication data for context-aware responses.
